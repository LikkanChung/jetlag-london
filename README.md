## Setup
1. Create a My Map on Google Maps
2. Import each file in [/maps](/maps) as a new layer
3. Configure stations and zones layers to have a uniform display style
4. Configure postcode layer to Styled by visibility, based on the `group` column
5. Configure other layers as desired

# Hiding Locations
The 206 included stations are all found on the [Transport for London Standard Tune map](https://tfl.gov.uk/maps/track/tube) and are all in Zone 1-2 (including stations in Zone 2/3).

The included lines are
* All Unerground Lines
* Overground Lines
    * except Liberty Line (as it's outside of Zone 1-2)
* Elizabeth Line
* Thameslink
* DLR

Other National Rail services (e.g. Southern only stations, e.g. South Bermondsey, are excluded).

Most stations which show internal interchanges are one location, while external interchanges are usually separated. Some stations are combined or separated discretionally. 
* Paddington - 1 station
* Hammersmith - 1 station
* Shepherd's Bush - 1 station
* Canary Wharf - 1 station
* West Hempstead - 1 station
* Liverpool St and Moorgate - 2 stations
    * Liverpool Street - Northern, Elizabeth Line, Weaver Line, Hammersmith & City, Circle, Metropolitan
    * Moorgate - Hammersmith & City, Circle, Metropolitan
* Edgware Road - 2 stations (as per map)
* Bank and Monument - 2 stations
    * Bank - Central, Waterloo & City, Noerhern, DLR
    * Monument - Circle, District
* Farringon & Barbican - 2 stations
    * Elizabeth Line is Farringdon only
* Hackney Downs and Hackney Central - 2 stations

Some stations have been named as explicitly different stations. For the purposes of the game, they are named as follows:
* _Bethnal Green (Weaver)_ and _Bethnal Green (Central)_
* _Edgware Road (Bakerloo)_ and _Edgware Road (Circle)_ 

## Administrative Regions
1. London Boroughs and City of London
2. Outward Postal Area (e.g. SW)
3. Outward Postal District (e.g. SW1A)
4. Inward Postal Sector (e.g. SW1A 1)

In the map, if displayed grouped, then postal areas (N, NW, E, etc) can be used as a different adminstrative division than postal districts (N1, N4, N5, etc).

For the 4th level Matching question, you can check Google Maps by pinned locations.

## Airports
Only London Heathrow and London City airports are included, as all locations are closest to these two, and none are closer to other "London" airports. 

However, the divide is relatively far west, where about 12% of hiding zones are closer to Heathrow. 

This question can be excluded if preferred.

## High Speed Rail Line
This has been excluded, as we have decided not to include this Measuring question in our game. 

## Rail Station
For the purpose of the Measuring question, Rail stations are determined as the London Station Group.
